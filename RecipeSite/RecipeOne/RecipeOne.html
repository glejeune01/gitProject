Vanilla Fruit Cake
60 Minutes
8 Servings
Instructions
  • Mix cake mix with vanilla extract
  • Prepare vanilla icing
  • Bake cake for 30 minutes
  • Apply icing to the cake and apply fruits
Ingredients
  • Vanilla cake mix
  • Your favorite fruits
  • Vanilla icing
  • Vanilla extract
