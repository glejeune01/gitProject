Rainbow Bowl
45 Minutes
4 Servings
Instructions
  • Roast chickpeas, peppers, and squash for 20 minutes
  • Drain beans, and cut lemons and avocado
  • Mix ingrediants into a salad bowl
  • Drizzle balsamic vinagrette for taste
Ingredients
  • Chickpeas, Beans,
  • Avocado, Red peppers,
  • Balsamic vinagrette,
  • Lemons, Squash
