Margherita Pizza
90 Minutes
6 Servings
Instructions
  • Flatten and spread the dough on a floured surface
  • Place crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce
  • Spread mozzarella liberally on pizza
  • Cook for 60 minutes and place basil on pizza
Ingredients
  • Pizza dough
  • Crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil
